Short Bio

I received a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Changchun University of Science and Technology (Jilin University now), China, in 1999. From 2004 to 2006, I studied in the System software Lab. of Myongji University, Korea, for a Master degree under the supervision of Prof. Yeonseung Ryu. In 2013, I received a PhD degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Korea University, Korea, under the supervision of Prof. Hoh Peter In and Prof. Minho Jo. In 2014, I joined the school of Information Engineering at Nanchang Institute of Technology.

Research

My research interests include

  • Information Centric Networks

  • Reinforcement Learning

  • Network Optimization

  • Multimedia Wireless Communications

  • Wireless Networks
